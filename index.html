About Me

I am 22 years old, currently in 2nd year (Graduating 2028), pursuing a career in Data Science from IIT Madras. I hail from West Bengal, India. Born and brought up in Medinipur, India. I have done my schooling from ViveKananda Mission High School in 2023, with Science as my background.

My idea behind taking up Data Science is my love for programming & Maths, and the fact that, given the knowledge, I can make a computer do almost anything I want it to do with my Codes -- focused on turning AI ideas into scalable web solutions .

I have a keen interest in the field of Machine Learning and Data Science, and I'm presently learning and working on the skills required to expertise in the same.

My hobbies
include playing FPS games, such as Call of Duty and Counter-Strike , Valorant; reading Tech articles, browsing YouTube recommendations sometime, traveling, and binge watching series.

I am a firm believer of passion and determination can lead you to success.



Words that Define Me - collaborative, diplomatic, deeply curious and adaptable.
